Gym Rules

  • Always wipe benches after use if sweating.
  • Return all dumbbells, plates and equipment to their designated places after use.
  • Handle gym equipment responsibly.
  • Any intentional damage to gym property may result in a penalty/fine.
  • Maintain discipline and respect fellow members.
  • Carry separate shoes and slippers for hygiene and safety.
